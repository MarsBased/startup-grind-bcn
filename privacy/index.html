Privacy Policy

Your data is yours

Following the Ley Orgánica 15/1999 de Protección de Datos Personales (from now on "LOPD"), all of your personal data submitted to http://www.startupgrind.cat/, sub-domains and corporate profiles in the social networks (from now on "Startup Grind Barcelona") will be processed and incorporated to our registration database, property of Startup Grind Barcelona, currently being registered in the Registro General de la Agencia Española de Protección de Datos, whose ultimate aim is the fiscal, economic and administrative management of the contractual relationship and the diffusion of commercial information of our products & services.

We inform you, that you are entitled to access, correct, cancel and oppose to our registry data as established by the aforementioned Law through e-mail at abacardit AT startupgrind.cat (subject "[LOPD] Personal data privacy.")

Security measures

Your personal data will be treated by Startup Grind Barcelona under the most strict confidentiality, applying the corresponding security measures, both technical and organizational, as established by the Real Decreto 1720/2007, del 21 de Diciembre, approved by the Reglamento de desarrollo de la Ley Orgánica 15/1999, del 13 de Diciembre, that concerns the treatment of personal data.

If you're under 14 years old, read this!

People under 14 years of age should not give out their personal data to Startup Grind Barcelona without the previous consent of their parents or legal tutors. Please, if you're under 14, ask your parents or legal tutors to contact Startup Grind Barcelona and we will do our best to help you out! :)

Visibility of your data

Your registration data for the Startup Grind Barcelona newsletter is kept private.

We use MailChimp to manage our newsletter subscriptions. You might want to read MailChimp's privacy policy for a better understanding of the tool.

Startup Grind Barcelona data

Because we respect your data, its integrity and security, we ask you to do so with ours. If you want to use our material and/or content, ask for permission before using it and we can talk like grown-ups :)
